The Story

From LA rooftops to
remote automation

I'm 25, Armenian, living in Barcelona. I spent 3.5 years as a PM at a big company. Quit. Tried a bunch of things — smart restaurant menus, an interview simulator, wine subscriptions. Some worked a little. None worked enough.

Then I found home services. I flew to Los Angeles, bought the tools, and started installing security cameras and smart home systems myself. Climbed on roofs. Ran cables through attics. Did the job so I could understand the job.

Once I understood the business end to end — lead, quote, schedule, install, collect — I automated it. Built an AI pipeline that catches incoming leads, researches the customer, writes a personalized response, and sends it back. Under 60 seconds. No human login needed.

Now I operate from Barcelona. Local technicians execute the jobs. The AI handles acquisition. I handle the system.

2019 Started in corporate. Product management, 3.5 years. Quit to build.
2022 First ventures — smart menus, interview sim, wine import. Learned what doesn't scale.
2024 Flew to LA. Installed cameras with my own hands. Learned the trade.
2025 Built the AI pipeline. Moved back to Barcelona. Business runs remotely.
What I'm Building

Novanest Pro
