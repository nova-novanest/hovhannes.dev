The non-obvious truth
The hard part is not doing the work.

The hard part is getting the next job while you’re still doing the last one. Skilled people lose not because they are bad at the craft, but because they are busy doing it when the next lead arrives.

Novanest Pro is the first instance of a larger idea: a personal AI operating layer for solo service workers. The AI should get the work, win the work, coordinate it, and let the pro just do the craft. The AI runs the business. The craftsman does the craft.
Why me
I learned the market from inside the job.

I did not start with software. I started by physically doing installations in Los Angeles, losing the same jobs other pros lose, and reverse-engineering what was actually happening. That is why the product is not a generic AI wrapper. It comes from the bottleneck I felt myself.
